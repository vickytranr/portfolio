Vicky Tran is a designer based in Markham, Ontario specializing in digital design. Currently a student majoring in Graphic Design at OCADU, she aims to create designs that are impactful and accessible, and is always looking for opportunities to continuously learn more so she can create dynamic designs with a user-centric approach. She is adaptable, curious, and always open to learning about new ideas, styles, and points of view.

Proficient in the Adobe Suite (Ps, Ai, Id, Pr, Ae) and expanding her skills into new tools like Blender, she aims to combine her passion for games with design for a future career doing UI/UX art and design within the game industry.
